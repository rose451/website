drought estimation by the ocean wind and ocean currents,Kwale County

Drought considered to be one of the major natural hazards causing destructive impact on the environment as well as the economy of countries throughout the world(WMO & GWD, 2016). In Kenya it has been termed as natural disaster after floods. Its almost impossible to avoid drought although drought preparedness can be developed and drought impact can be managed, this however will depend on how well drought are defined and drought characteristics quantified.Remote sensing data in combination with other climate and weather data can play an important role in estimating the influence of ocean winds and current to their environs and the relationship between their behavior and drought experienced at coastal part more specifically at Kwale County.The main aim of this study is to show effect and influence of ocean waves, wind, temperatures and current to their surroundings and their contribution towards drought. The influence of hot air and cold air from ocean and other ocean variations towards drought estimation
